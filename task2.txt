
Relative Positioning:

Element stays in its normal position in the flow of the document.
You can move it from that normal position using top, right, bottom, and left.
Other elements are not affected by its movement.

Absolute Positioning:

Element is taken out of the normal flow.
Positioned relative to its nearest positioned ancestor or the document body.
Can overlap with other elements; it doesn't affect their layout, and they don't affect its position.
